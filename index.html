How to use Local Lift

Discover and support local businesses in Lake Forest, Illinois.

  • Search — type in the search box to filter businesses instantly by name, category, or keyword.
  • Filter by category — tap a category pill to show only that type of business.
  • Filter & sort by rating — show only highly-rated spots, or order results by Top rated, Featured, or Name (A–Z).
  • View details — open any business to see its hours, contact info, a map, its deal, and reviews.
  • Sign in with Google — required before writing anything. Google’s 2-step verification keeps out bots.
  • Reviews & ratings — once signed in, leave a star rating and review on any business.
  • Bookmark favorites — tap the ☆ on a card to save it. Saved businesses appear under Favorites.
  • Recommendations — favorite at least two businesses and Local Lift suggests more you’ll like on your Favorites page.
  • Claim deals — open a business and claim its deal to reveal the coupon code.
  • PDF Report — generate a printable report of your saved businesses and recommendations from the top bar.
